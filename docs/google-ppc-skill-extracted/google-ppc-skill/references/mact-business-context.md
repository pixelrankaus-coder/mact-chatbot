# MACt Business Context for PPC Recommendations

## Company Profile

**MACt (Mining & Cement Technology)** is a GFRC (Glass Fibre Reinforced Concrete) manufacturer based in Australia. The business operates B2B, serving architects, builders, developers, and construction companies.

## Product Categories

### Primary Products
- **Architectural Panels**: Facade cladding, feature walls
- **Custom Elements**: Bespoke architectural features
- **Commercial Solutions**: Large-scale building facades
- **Residential Applications**: Smaller-scale installations

### Product Characteristics
- High-value orders (typically $5,000 - $500,000+)
- Custom manufacturing (made-to-order)
- Long lead times (weeks to months)
- Technical specification required

## Target Audience

### Primary Segments
1. **Architects**: Specify products, influence decision
2. **Commercial Builders**: Large project volumes
3. **Developers**: Multi-unit residential, commercial
4. **Facade Contractors**: Installation specialists

### Buying Behavior
- Research-intensive (multiple touchpoints)
- Specification-driven decisions
- Price is secondary to quality/capability
- Relationship-based purchasing
- Project-based buying cycles

## Geographic Focus

### Primary Markets
- **New South Wales**: Sydney metro, regional
- **Victoria**: Melbourne, growing market
- **Queensland**: Brisbane, Gold Coast

### Secondary Markets
- South Australia
- Western Australia (mining/commercial)

## Seasonality Patterns

### Peak Periods
- **Q1 (Jan-Mar)**: Project planning, specification phase
- **Q2 (Apr-Jun)**: Construction season ramp-up
- **Q3 (Jul-Sep)**: Peak construction activity

### Slower Periods
- **Q4 (Oct-Dec)**: Holiday slowdown, budget planning

### Weather Considerations
- Construction slows during heavy rain periods
- Summer heat can affect installation schedules

## Competitive Landscape

### Direct Competitors
- Other GFRC manufacturers
- Precast concrete suppliers
- Alternative facade systems (ACM, terracotta)

### Competitive Advantages
- Australian manufacturing
- Custom design capability
- Technical support and expertise
- Quality certifications

## PPC Strategy Implications

### Keyword Themes (High Intent)
- "GFRC panels Australia"
- "concrete facade panels"
- "architectural precast panels"
- "commercial building facade"
- "custom concrete cladding"

### Negative Keywords
- DIY terms
- Residential concrete (slabs, driveways)
- Mining equipment (different MACt meaning)

### Landing Page Alignment
- Product galleries with project photos
- Technical specifications
- Quote request forms
- Case studies by project type

### Conversion Goals
1. **Primary**: Quote/enquiry form submissions
2. **Secondary**: Phone calls
3. **Tertiary**: Brochure downloads, sample requests

### Budget Allocation Guidance
- Prioritize high-intent commercial terms
- Brand campaigns for awareness
- Retargeting for long sales cycles
- Geographic targeting by state

## AI Recommendation Filters

When generating recommendations, consider:

1. **Lead Quality > Volume**: A few qualified leads beat many unqualified
2. **Long Sales Cycle**: Don't judge campaign too quickly
3. **Seasonal Adjustments**: Expect fluctuations by quarter
4. **Geographic Relevance**: Focus on serviceable areas
5. **Product Alignment**: Match keywords to actual product capabilities
6. **B2B Metrics**: CPA expectations differ from B2C
7. **Cross-Reference Cin7**: Which products actually sell vs. which get clicks
